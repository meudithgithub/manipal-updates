What is Manipal Updates ?

Founded in 2019, Manipal Updates is an independent student organisation run by a team of dedicated students from Manipal. We aim to help you with staying updated with everything happening around you! We also have a student marketplace, a digital Lost & Found community, and a dedicated platform for updates by student clubs of Manipal. We also help local businesses & startups with their marketing & promotional requirements. DM us if you would like to collaborate!
